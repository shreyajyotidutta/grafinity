Hey Creative Beans, Here how it works

Step 1 - Getting to know each other


In this stage we discuss and clarify the goals, objective, timeline of the project over calls or face-to-face meetings.



Step 2 - Market Research


In this stage we research the market and target audience and position the brand that helps the brand grow in this competitive market.



Step 3 - User Profile Generation


In this stage, we use previous findings to sketch out user profiles of the target market.



Step 4: Brand Attributes


Brand attributes are the keywords that portrays extrinsic and intrinsic properties of the brand. It reveals the brand's abstract and physical attributes.



Step 5: Sharing and exploring options


In this stage, we create a few moodboards and stylescapes and scrutinize the concepts and sort out what aligns with the objective that has been previously decided.



Step 6: Logo and Identity Design


In this stage, we research the industry and target market and collaboratively build a logo and identity that justifies and reflects your brand's awesome image.



Step 7: Iterate and finalisation


After choosing from 2-3 options, this is the moment to do the subtle changes and finalise the logo and identity with it.



Step 8: Voice of your Brand


Language is as important as aesthetics for a brand. We also help you define your verbal identity.



Step 9: Transfer of Documents

  • All file formats needed.
